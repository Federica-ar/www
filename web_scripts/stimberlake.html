Research Interests

I like to try to imagine how microbes can adapt to an environment - on all time scales - and how they interact with their friends, enemies, and hosts. It amazes me how life has indelibly changed our planet, and I'd love to get a better understanding of how microbial community metabolism molds the inorganic world.

Past Life
