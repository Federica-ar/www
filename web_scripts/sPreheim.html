Sarah Pacocha Preheim

Research Interests

I am interested in bacterial diversity within aquatic ecosystems. The small size, genetic diversity and metabolic versatility of bacteria make them ideal systems for studying principles in ecology and evolution.
Microbial community structure: How the metabolism of individual organisms fit into the function of the community as a whole Genetic events involved in ecological differentiation: Identifying genes in the genome of closely related bacteria that are responsible for differential environmental distribution Interspecies metabolic interactions: Identifying metabolic interdependencies in natural communities
